Who Are We?

We're a community of communities ~ a network of doers, makers, builders, growers, tinkerers, chefs, scientists, and the chronically curious. We come together and build as a community; making space for the wild ideas people have been saving for "someday."

The Banana Rodeo is the annual showcase. La Finka is the project — a year-round community-building experiment in Reno, NV. One part community garden. One part cultural-hub. One part living-lab.

The Banana Mission:
Spark collective imagination and empower joyful experimentation ~ building the world we want to live in, together.

The Banana is inside us all.

We value local economies, permaculture practices, and responsible consumption. We leave things better than we found them.

What We've Done

Celebrating 5 Years of Banana Rodeo

Five years in. Some of what's happened:

  • Creative platform — weird, wonderful art alongside experimental science and engineering
  • The BBB (Banana Business Bureau) — the grassroots crew that dreams this up each year
  • 15+ small businesses across Reno and beyond have sponsored, tabled, or co-created with us
  • 501(c)(3) backing — fiscally sponsored by the Beauty Project Foundation. Legitimate, tax-deductible
  • Community Garden 2026 — y'all helped us hit our $5,000 goal to launch the Community Garden with Urban Roots & Humilitree right here in Reno

We're doing the thing. What's next?

Why Banana Rodeo?

No spectators — only contributors.

Sponsoring shows up in a context people actually love. Our crowd is curious, creative, community-minded — and they notice the businesses that show up for the things they care about.

  • Community-first. No corporate overlords, no gatekeeping.
  • Weird in the best way. Sponsors get to be part of something genuinely memorable.
  • Diverse and multigenerational — a crowd that doesn't show up anywhere else in Reno.
  • Growing. Each year brings more attention, more intention, more buzz.
  • Sponsors as partners, not logos.
