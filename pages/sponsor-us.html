Who Are We?

We're a community of communities — a network of doers, makers, builders, growers, tinkerers, chefs, scientists, and the chronically curious. We make space for the wild ideas people have been saving for "someday." To come together and build as a community.

The Banana Rodeo is the showcase. La Finka is the project — a year-round community-building experiment in Reno, NV. One part community garden. One part cultural-hub. One part living-lab. All parts curiosity.

The Banana Mission: spark collective imagination and empower joyful experimentation — building the world we want to live in, together.

We value local economies, permaculture practices, and responsible consumption. We try to leave things better than we found them.

How You Can Support Us

A few ways in. We'd rather start with a conversation than a pitch deck.

💸 Financial Sponsorship

Covers venue, infrastructure, accessibility, and keeps tickets affordable for everyone. We acknowledge sponsors meaningfully — logos, event recognition, tickets, booth space, co-created moments if the relationship calls for it. The exact shape depends on you.

🎁 In-Kind Support

Goods, services, food, printing, prizes. If you make something useful, we probably need it. In-kind partners get the same recognition as financial ones.

✨ Something Else

Collaborations, introductions, an idea we haven't thought of. We're listening.

La Finka is fiscally sponsored by the Beauty Project Foundation, a 501(c)(3). Contributions are tax-deductible.

Reach out. We'll figure out what fits.

Why Banana Rodeo?

Sponsoring shows up in a context people actually love. Our crowd is curious, creative, community-minded — and they notice the businesses that show up for the things they care about.

  • Community-first. No corporate overlords, no gatekeeping.
  • Weird in the best way. Sponsors get to be part of something genuinely memorable.
  • Diverse and multigenerational — a crowd that doesn't show up anywhere else in Reno.
  • Growing. Each year brings more attention, more intention, more buzz.
  • Sponsors as partners, not logos.
