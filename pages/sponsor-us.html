Who Are We?

The Banana Rodeo Science Fair is a community-powered event in Reno, NV where curiosity meets creativity—and where the wild ideas people have been saving for "someday" finally get their moment in the sun.

We bring together artists, makers, tinkerers, scientists, chefs and anyone else who believes that asking weird questions is the beginning of something great.

Our mission: spark collective imagination and empower joyful experimentation by creating space for people to gather, tinker, and build the world they want to live in—together.

By valuing local economies, permaculture practices, and responsible consumption, we strive to leave a positive impact on our community and the planet. One peel at a time.

How You Can Support Us

Every contribution makes the rodeo ride a little smoother.

Important: La Finka is a fiscally sponsored project by The Beauty Project Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Your donation is tax-deductible.

💸 Financial Sponsorship

Financial sponsors help us cover the real costs of putting on an event: venue, infrastructure, printing, accessibility, and keeping ticket prices low for everyone. In return, we offer:

  • Logo placement on our website, event signage, and printed materials
  • Recognition in event announcements
  • Complimentary tickets to the event for your team
  • Table/booth space at the event (higher tiers)
  • Co-branded content and a feature in our community recap (higher tiers)

Sponsorship tiers start at any amount that feels right. We believe in flexible, relationship-based partnerships — reach out and let's figure out what works for both of us.

🎁 In-Kind Sponsorship

Can't do cash? No problem. In-kind sponsors provide goods or services that help the event run. Past in-kind contributions have included:

  • Food and beverages for attendees or volunteers
  • Printing services (signage, programs, ribbons, labels)
  • Equipment or supplies for exhibitors
  • Gift baskets or prizes for award winners

In-kind sponsors receive the same community recognition as financial sponsors. It all adds up.
