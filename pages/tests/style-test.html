Banana Rodeo Returns

By Our Staff Reporter

The annual Banana Rodeo Science Fair is set to return to Reno this spring, bringing with it a full roster of eccentric experiments, improbable hypotheses, and at least one volcano.

Organizers promise a bigger venue, more bananas, and a mystery keynote that sources describe only as "transcendent."

Sponsors Sought

By Our Business Desk

The Science Fair committee is actively seeking sponsors for the 2026 event. "We need microscopes, we need funds, and frankly we need more bananas," said one organizer who declined to be named.

Sponsorship tiers range from "Potassium Pal" to the coveted "Grand Banana" level.

Confessional Opens

By Our Culture Reporter

For the first time, attendees will have the opportunity to confess their banana-related sins in a dedicated Banana Confessional Phone Booth.

"We've all done something with a banana we're not proud of," said one anonymous attendee. "Now we can make peace with it."
