What is this?

It's not just a pool party, it's a party with a purpose.

The Banana Rodeo is a community-driven, absurdist banana-themed pool party fostering joy & connection, through silly educational activities centered around the Humble Banana.

We cultivate a safe space for creative expression, knowledge sharing, and celebrating our uniqueness.

The Banana Rodeo includes ToDo:: Links [cowboy cosplay], [ridiculous rodeo competitions] (judged by a distinguished [Bananel] of Clowns), and a [science fair] focused on sustainability (serious and absurd submissions encouraged).

We've organized a whole day of activities including such competitions as Banana Riding, Barrel Racing, and the Best Banana Dish.

Intermixed throughout the day there will be side adventures, activities and art curated by our own community.
