What is this?

It's not just a pool party, it's a party with a purpose.

The Banana Rodeo is a community-driven, absurdist banana-themed pool party fostering joy & connection, through silly educational activities centered around the Humble Banana.

We cultivate a safe space for creative expression, knowledge sharing, and celebrating our uniqueness.
