# Banana Meditation

A forced-meditation page. The conceit: "We're Not Loading — This is a Meditation." Visitors encounter a guided banana peeling experience before they can continue. Absurd, calming, and very on-brand.

## Concept

A page that refuses to hurry. It opens with a fake loading screen that reveals itself as intentional, then walks the visitor through a guided banana peeling meditation. Scrolls through a poem. Ends with a one-minute timer before releasing them.

## Key Questions

- Is this a standalone page or a loading screen that precedes another page?
- Does the one-minute timer enforce the wait, or is it skippable (honor system)?
- Should there be ambient audio — breathing, nature sounds, Handel's Messiah?
- How does the scroll pacing work — auto-scroll, user-driven, timed?

## Rough UX / Notes

- Opening: fake loading bar stalls, then text reveals "We're Not Loading — This is a forced meditation"
- Transition into the poem with gentle scroll or fade
- One-minute timer at the end, styled as a meditation bell or countdown
- Minimal UI — let the text breathe

## Content: A Moment of Mindful Peeling

Take a deep breath in, feeling the sweetness of the moment, and exhale any distractions.

Hold your banana gently in your hands. Notice its color, its curve, its texture. This simple fruit has traveled far to be here with you, ripened under the sun, carrying the warmth of the earth in its peel.

Slowly begin to peel it, feeling the soft resistance as the skin separates. With each movement, let go of tension, like the banana shedding its outer layer. What parts of yourself are ready to be released today?

Pause and observe the fruit within—pure, nourishing, and ready to be received. As you take your first bite, savor the creamy sweetness. Let it fill you with gratitude for nature's generosity and the small joys of life.

Chew slowly. Feel the nourishment entering your body. This banana is more than just food—it is energy, it is life, it is a reminder that sometimes, the simplest things bring the greatest pleasure.

Take another deep breath in. Exhale with a smile. You are present, you are nourished, you are whole.

Now, enjoy your banana.
