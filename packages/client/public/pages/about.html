About Banana Rodeo

The Banana Rodeo is more than just a pool party - it's a celebration of creativity, community, and the humble banana. Born from a desire to bring people together in the most absurd and delightful way possible, our events combine the joy of summer pool parties with educational activities, art installations, and banana-themed everything.

Our Mission

We aim to create a safe, inclusive space where creativity knows no bounds. Through our events, we:

  • Foster community connections through shared experiences
  • Promote artistic expression in all its forms
  • Educate through play and interactive experiences
  • Create lasting memories and friendships

The Banana Philosophy

Why bananas? Because they're the perfect metaphor for our community: individually unique, but better together. Like a bunch of bananas, we believe in the power of unity while celebrating our differences.
