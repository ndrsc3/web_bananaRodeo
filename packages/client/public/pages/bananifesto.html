Our Mission

We've got Purpose!
What's Yours?

To spark collective imagination and empower joyful experimentation by creating space for people to gather, tinker, and build the world they want to live in—together.

By valuing local economies, permaculture practices, and responsible consumption, we strive to leave a positive impact on our environment and community.

We believe that together, through playful engagement and shared experiences, we can cultivate a brighter and more sustainable future, one peel at a time.

Our Values

Purpose

We align our actions with the future we want to see—one that’s regenerative, just, and full of possibility.

Access

We work to reduce barriers—economic, cultural, or structural—to participation, creativity, and contribution.

Experimentation

We embrace the unknown and celebrate iterative learning, especially when it’s messy.

Imagination

We believe dreaming wildly is the first step toward building something real. Absurdity is welcome..

Care

We treat people, land, and ideas with reverence and responsibility. Growth only happens when things are nurtured.

Community

We center relationships, collaboration, and local stewardship—no one rides alone at the rodeo.

Impact & Initiatives

The Banana Rodeo aims to leave a lasting impact by:

  • Inspiring joy and connection within the community.
  • Supporting local businesses and fostering a thriving local economy.
  • Providing opportunities for learning, growth, and skill development.
  • Host an annual community science fair blending sustainability, art, and invention.
  • Provide a platform for local artists, builders, and organizers to share their work and ideas.
  • Support youth and multigenerational learning through hands-on demos and collaborative experiments.
  • Showcase DIY tech, permaculture, and regenerative practices.
  • Reduce waste and model low-impact event infrastructure.
  • Strengthen local networks through creative collaboration and shared purpose.
  • Partner with regional organizations to amplify community resilience and innovation.
  • Offer sliding scale and volunteer-based access to increase participation.
