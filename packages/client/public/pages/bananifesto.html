Our Mission

We've got Purpose!
What's Yours?

The Banana Rodeo is a community-driven, absurdist banana-themed pool party fostering joy & connection, through silly educational activities centered around the Humble Banana.

We cultivate a safe space for creative expression, knowledge sharing, and celebrating our uniqueness.

By valuing local economies, permaculture practices, and responsible consumption, we strive to leave a positive impact on our environment and community.

We believe that together, through playful engagement and shared experiences, we can cultivate a brighter and more sustainable future, one peel at a time.

Impact & Initiatives

The Banana Rodeo aims to leave a lasting impact by:

  • Inspiring joy and connection within the community.
  • Raising awareness about sustainability and responsible consumption.
  • Supporting local businesses and fostering a thriving local economy.
  • Providing opportunities for learning, growth, and skill development.
  • Celebrating the unique contributions of individuals and fostering a sense of belonging.
