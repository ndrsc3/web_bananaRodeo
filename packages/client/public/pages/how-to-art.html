HOW DO I DO ART AT THE BANANA RODEO?

Well, bring yourself. Costumes and props can help cast your own unique bit, schtick, or character.

If by art, you mean objets d'art, as in 'things', that's great!! We can't wait to see what you bring.

Whichever direction you go, we want to help. There are a handful of guidelines we ask you to adhere to.

We very much encourage independent art and can support you by providing basic resources like power, placement, and other logistical help.

If you want to bring independent art to the party please fill out the form below so we can support you. Plus, we like to know who brought that cool thing so we can tell you how much we love it!

We highly value the spontaneous and renegade art brought by OUR COMMUNITY.

If you want some tips on how to pull this off, reach out to us.
Thank you to our fellow inspirations at The Tupperware parties where we pulled some of this content.
