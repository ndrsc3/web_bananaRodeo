How to Art

It's All Art!

No, Really... How can I art?

The Banana Rodeo isn’t just something you attend—it’s something you bring to life. We believe everyone has a little weird brilliance inside them, and we want to see it out in the world.

We highly value the spontaneous and renegade art brought by OUR COMMUNITY.

Whichever direction you go, we want to help.

Have an idea for a sculpture, character, roaming performance, interactive experiment, or just something that needs to exist?
This is your invitation to make it real.

Bring Your Own:

  • Objects of mystery
  • Interactive creations, installations, or experiments
  • Costumed chaos, roving weirdos, tiny portals
  • Things that make people feel, wonder, laugh, or question what it is to banana

There are no wrong ideas—just stuff we haven’t seen yet.

If you want some tips on how to pull this off, reach out to us.
