"It's Just a Science Fair!"

What is this?

At its core, The Banana Rodeo is an open invitation to: share space, show up authentically, and try things that don't quite make sense—yet somehow do. It's not a festival, not a party, not an art show and not truly "just a science fair"... yet it has the best parts of all.

The Banana Rodeo hosted at La Finka is a living showcase of our community— featuring a hands-on science fair as a vibrant platform for artists, builders, organizers, and misfits of all kinds to connect, gather, and co-create.

What began as a spontaneous experiment is now, four years in, a flagship gathering for a crew of intentional, future-facing weirdos.

There are no spectators at The Banana Rodeo—only contributors. It's where people come to test ideas, shed expectations, and explore what community could look like when it's guided by values rather than structure.

Join us in filling our container with temporary worlds in a shared space that honors both chaos and care. Community, Imagination, & Purpose are not just buzzwords—they're the blueprint.

It's about to get buckin' bananas.
