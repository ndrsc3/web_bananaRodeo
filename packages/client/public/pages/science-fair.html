IT'S JUST A SCIENCE FAIR

The Banana Rodeo Science Fair is a dynamic gathering that celebrates both serious science and the joy of silly experimentation.

We bring together experts and enthusiasts alike, fostering an inclusive space where everyone, regardless of background or expertise, is encouraged to participate.

Through a blend of educational exhibits, playful activities, and hands-on workshops, we aim to demystify science and inspire creativity in exploring sustainable practices.

Our mission is to empower individuals from all walks of life to contribute to a greener future, fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and innovation.

Join us in embracing the fun side of science and making meaningful strides towards a more sustainable world together.
