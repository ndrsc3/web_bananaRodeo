IT'S JUST A SCIENCE FAIR

The Banana Rodeo Science Fair is a dynamic gathering that celebrates both serious science and the joy of silly experimentation.

We bring together experts and enthusiasts alike, fostering an inclusive space where everyone, regardless of background or expertise, is encouraged to participate.

Through a blend of educational exhibits, playful activities, and hands-on workshops, we aim to demystify science and inspire creativity in exploring sustainable practices.

Our mission is to empower individuals from all walks of life to contribute to a greener future, fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and innovation.

Join us in embracing the fun side of science and making meaningful strides towards a more sustainable world together.

Entry Information

How Are Entries Judged?

Your entry will be judged by how well it follows the Scientific Method™. Each entry will be judged in a point system (1-5) for the following categories:

1. Is/are the question(s)/hypothesis well-defined?

2. Does the project test the hypothesis by performing an experiment or experiments and collects data in a reproducible manner?

3. Are any analytical or empirical mathematical expressions used to analyze/support the observed data?

4. Is there any use of graphs, serious or silly, to support the data/observations?

5. Does/Do the conclusion(s) support/answer the original question(s)/hypothesis?

6. Are appropriate references used that support the findings/observations?

7. How creative is the project?

8. Least use of AI?

What Do I Prepare?

Prepare a poster/diorama/tri-fold with the following dimensions in mind:

1. Width 24” - 40” (60.96 cm - 101.6 cm) with a height of 28” - 36” (71.12 cm - 91.44 cm)...or whatever.

2. Include your name and the title of your project.

3. You are encouraged to use recycled/repurposed materials.

What Awards are Being Judged?

Prepare a poster/diorama/tri-fold with the following dimensions in mind:

1. Best in Show

2. Best in Category

3. Best Use of Recycled Materials

4. Best Use of AI

5. Best Silliness

6. Best Use of La Finka

What Can I Expect Day-Of?

Upon arrival, there will be a designated area for your entry.

Judging of the entries will take place during the event at a scheduled time by the Distinguished Bananel.

It’s not required (but encouraged) to be physically next to your entry to explain your entry.

You may be asked to present your entry to the judges.
