Science is Cool!

THE BANANA RODEO SCIENCE FAIR IS WHERE CURIOSITY MEETS CREATIVITY—AND WHERE THE WILD IDEAS YOU'VE BEEN SAVING FOR "SOMEDAY" FINALLY GET THEIR MOMENT IN THE SUN.

We believe science isn't limited to labs or textbooks. It's in backyard experiments, solar-powered art, DIY tech, fermentation, kitchen chemistry, and bizarre contraptions that (somehow) work.

Whether you're building something beautiful, solving a real-world problem, or just asking a weird question no one else is asking—you belong here.

This is an open invitation to showcase your curiosity, your research, your side project, or your almost-product that just needs a nudge.

We aim to demystify science and inspire creativity in exploring sustainable practices to empower individuals from all walks of life to contribute to a greener future, fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and innovation.

Bring your obsessions. Bring your experiments. Let's make meaningful strides towards a more sustainable world.
Let's science together!

Entry Information


How Are Entries Judged?

Your entry will be judged by how well it follows the Scientific Method. Each entry will be judged in a point system (1 - 5) for the following categories:

1. Is/are the question(s)/hypothesis well-defined?

2. Does the project test the hypothesis by performing an experiment or experiments and collects data in a reproducible manner?

3. Are any analytical or empirical mathematical expressions used to analyze/support the observed data?

4. Is there any use of graphs, serious or silly, to support the data/observations?

5. Does/Do the conclusion(s) support/answer the original question(s)/hypothesis?

6. Are appropriate references used that support the findings/observations?

7. Creativity - Is it Novel? Surprising? Delightfully Strange?

8. Least use of AI?

What Do I Prepare?

Prepare a poster/diorama/tri-fold with the following dimensions in mind:


1. Width 24" - 40" (60.96 cm - 101.6 cm) with a height of 28" - 36" (71.12 cm - 91.44 cm)...or whatever.

2. Include your name and the title of your project.

3. You are encouraged to use recycled/repurposed materials.

What Awards are Being Judged?

1. First Prize: Best in Show

2. Least use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology

3. 3rd Prime Prize - What is this?

4. Most Creative

5. Most likely to be used at La Finka

What Can I Expect Day-Of?

Arrive no later than 1pm to set up, there will be a designated area for your entry.

Judging of the entries will take place during the event by the distinguished Potassium Powerhouse.

It's not required (but encouraged) to be physically next to your entry to explain your entry to Dr. York Smith.

You may be asked to present your entry to the judges.
