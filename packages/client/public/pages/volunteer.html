So You Want to Banana?

Join the Rodeo Crew!

The Banana Rodeo doesn’t happen on its own... It’s made possible by the many hands, hearts, and minds who show up to build it. Whether you're a spreadsheet wrangler, a signage slinger, a member of the guerilla marketers, or just good at showing up when it counts, there's a spot for you on the Rodeo Crew.


We're looking for volunteers to help:

  • Prep the rodeo grounds (building, painting, decoring, tinkering)
  • Support the science fair (setup, check-in, molding minds)
  • Keep the clowns clowning
  • Keep the vibes flowing (music, MCs, clownsfloaters, floaters of floaters)
  • Handle logistics (trash, Recycling, compost, signage, water, radios, oh my)
  • Tidy up post-rodeo (the noble, magical cool-cats clean-up)

No experience needed just a willingness to show up, get your hands dirty, and help make the magic real.
